VR View is the name used by Google for its product/technology used to embed immersive 360 and 3D content.

The web version of Google VR View was created primarily by and is maintained by Boris Smus.*

VR View on vrview.io (this site) is now based on a fork of the Boris Smus version and has the same open source licence. (Apache-2)

* Boris Smus is also the primary creator and maintainer of the WebVR Boilerplate and WebVR Polyfill, both of which are used in VR View.
