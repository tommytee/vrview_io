VR View is the name used by Google for its product/technology used to embed immersive 360 and 3D content.

The web version of Google VR View was created primarily by and is maintained by Boris Smus

VR View on vrview.io (this site) is now based on the Google/Boris Smus version.

This version includes a server, some video player updates, and an experimental connect mode that allows the use inexpensive cell phones to view 3D 360 content.
